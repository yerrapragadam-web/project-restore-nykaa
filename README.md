# Project Restore — Solving First-Order Churn at Nykaa

> *"Nykaa wins the first order but loses the relationship."*

## What is this?
A full product strategy to reduce first-time buyer churn at Nykaa — 
from primary user research and competitor analysis to RICE-prioritised 
solutions, wireframes, and a live interactive prototype.

## The Problem
60% of first-time Nykaa buyers cited no relevant follow-up as the reason 
they didn't return. 3 out of 5 users received no post-purchase communication 
that felt relevant.

**Root causes identified:**
| Problem | Why it happens |
|---|---|
| Discovery Overload | Too many choices, no guidance for first-time buyers |
| No Replenishment Triggers | Lack of timely nudges for reorders |
| Loyalty Feels Transactional | Benefits kick in late, not early in the relationship |
| No Personalization | Generic homepage with no memory of past behavior |

## Research
- **Primary:** Survey of 20 first-time Nykaa buyers
- **Secondary:** 500 most recent App Store reviews (avg 3.6★, 35% are 1–2★) + Reddit r/IndianSkincareAddicts
- **Competitor analysis:** Tira, Sephora, Purple, KindLife, Foxy, Meesho

## Solutions (RICE Prioritised)

| Solution | RICE Score | Priority |
|---|---|---|
| Personalized Post-Purchase Routine Builder | 216 | Highest |
| "What To Buy Next" Recommendation Engine | 231 | Highest |
| First Repeat Purchase Discount + Skin Quiz | 340 | High |
| Loyalty Points with Visible Progress Bar | 324 | High |

**North Star Metric:** 30-Day Repeat Purchase Rate for First-Time Buyers

## Projected Impact
A 5pp uplift in 30-day repeat purchase rate can unlock:
- **+30 Lakh** incremental buyers annually
- **+₹540 Cr** incremental revenue
- **+₹135 Cr** incremental contribution
